Paneer Tikka

Delicious grilled paneer marinated in Indian spices.

Ingredients

  • Paneer - 200g
  • Yogurt - 1/2 cup
  • Spices (Turmeric, Red Chili, Garam Masala) - 1 tsp each
  • Lemon Juice - 1 tbsp
  • Bell Peppers & Onions - Chopped

Steps

  1. Cut paneer into cubes.
  2. Mix yogurt with spices and lemon juice.
  3. Marinate paneer and vegetables for 1 hour.
  4. Grill or bake at 200Â°C for 15 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with mint chutney.
